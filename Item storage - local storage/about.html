Memory sports

My interest in memory was inspired by Tony Buzan. He has written many popular books on how to utilise memory in day to day life. Through his writings I discovered the World Memory Championships.

Tony Buzan

Tony Buzan wrote about and popularised mnemonic systems as a means of improving education and creativity. He is credited with inventing the mind mapping system which enables an individual to record information in a map structure for easy recollection. In 1991 he initiated the World Memory Championships. As of 2022, this competition has been running for 31 years

World Memory Championships

Initially participation was small, and the contestants were mainly based in Europe. One of the early champions was Dominic O'Brien who was crowned champion 8 times and went on to have a successful career as an a memory expert, including authoring multiple books and appearing as a TV personality. In recent times participation has grown and participants from more than 30 countries participate in the annual event.
